The Person Behind the Page

Hello, I'm Evelina. I want to share a bit of my own journey with you.

Not too long ago, I was trapped in the cycle of seeking validation from others, battling self-doubt, and believing happiness came from external approval. But a pivotal moment changed everything. I realized that true happiness begins with self-acceptance.

This journey of embracing my uniqueness, flaws and all, led me to become a psychologist. Along this transformative path, I made a deliberate choice to pursue higher education, earning my Bachelor's degree in Psychology.

My thirst for understanding the human mind and the complexities of our emotions led me to further studies. I completed my Master's degree in Clinical Psychology, specializing in self-esteem, self-love, and the treatment of depression.

I'm here to help you navigate your own path to self-acceptance because I've been there, and I understand. Together, we can discover your inner strength and resilience.
